How to Use Exam Portal Offline

Method 1: Using Acode (Recommended)

  1. Install "Acode" from Play Store
  2. Open Acode app
  3. Click the three dots menu (â‹®)
  4. Select "Open Folder"
  5. Navigate to where you saved the exam files
  6. Click on "index.html"
  7. Click the "Live Server" button (looks like a play button)
  8. Your exam portal will open in your browser!

Method 2: Using QuickEdit

  1. Install "QuickEdit" from Play Store
  2. Open QuickEdit app
  3. Click the menu button
  4. Select "Open File"
  5. Navigate to where you saved the exam files
  6. Click on "index.html"
  7. Click the "Preview" button
  8. Your exam portal will open in your browser!

Important Notes:

  • Make sure to keep all files in the same folder
  • Don't delete any files from the folder
  • The exam will work completely offline
  • You can close and reopen the app anytime
