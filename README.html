University of Haripur Exam Portal

Offline-First Exam System

How It Works:

  1. First Visit (Online Required):
    • Open the portal with internet connection
    • Register/login to your account
    • All necessary data is automatically cached
  2. Subsequent Visits (Fully Offline):
    • No internet required
    • Open the portal anytime
    • All features work offline
    • Exam results are saved locally

Key Features:

  • Works completely offline after first visit
  • No internet needed for exams
  • Results saved locally
  • Secure and reliable
  • Can be installed as a desktop app

Important Notes:

  • First visit must be online
  • Keep the app installed for offline access
  • All data is stored securely on your device
  • You can use the app without internet after first setup

Installation Guide:

  1. Visit the portal website
  2. Click "Add to Home Screen" (mobile) or "Install" (desktop)
  3. Follow the prompts to install
  4. Open the installed app
  5. Complete first-time setup with internet
  6. Ready to use offline!

Troubleshooting:

  • If the app doesn't work offline:
    • Make sure you completed first-time setup online
    • Try reinstalling the app
    • Clear browser cache and try again
  • If you see "No Internet Connection":
    • This is normal - the app works offline
    • Continue using the app as normal

Need Help? Contact your course instructor or the IT department for assistance.
