Install Exam Portal

Step 1: Open the App

Click the button below to open the Exam Portal:

Open Exam Portal

Step 2: Install on Android

1. Open the app in Chrome browser

2. Click the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right

3. Select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"

4. Click "Install" when prompted

Step 2: Install on iPhone

1. Open the app in Safari browser

2. Click the share button (□↑) at the bottom

3. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen"

4. Click "Add" when prompted

Important Notes:
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection for the first installation
  • After installation, the app will work offline
  • Your exam data will be saved on your device
  • Do not clear browser data after installation

Need Help?

If you have any issues installing the app, please contact your instructor.
